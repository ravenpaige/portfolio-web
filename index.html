About Me

Hi, I’m Terri Clark — a content strategist, systems integrator, and technical communicator who makes complex stuff make sense.

Over the past decade, I’ve helped engineering teams, repair divisions, and marketing departments work smarter by building better tools, writing clearer content, and connecting the dots between people and platforms.

I’m a systems thinker with a practical streak: sometimes I code custom PHP/MySQL or JavaScript apps, sometimes I configure a low-code form builder or app generator, and sometimes I just rewrite your documentation so people stop filing help tickets.

I work remotely from Cassopolis, Michigan, and I care deeply about sustainability, accessibility, and using technology to make life more manageable — not more complicated.

Lately, I’ve been diving into the fast-evolving world of AI — exploring how language models, symbolic systems, and human-centered tools can work together. Whether it's a product API or a philosophical architecture, I help make the invisible intelligible.

What I Believe

  • Clear is kind. Good documentation and intuitive workflows reduce stress, prevent errors, and help people do their best work.
  • Right tool, right job. Whether it’s custom code, a low-code platform, or just a better process, I choose what fits — not what’s trending.
  • The devil’s in the details — and that’s where I work. I believe small things make a big difference, and I take the time to get them right.
  • Systems shape experience. A broken form or a missing instruction isn’t minor — it’s a pain point. I fix those so people can focus on what matters.
  • Sustainability matters. In tools, in teams, and in life. I design systems that last, support growth, and don’t burn people out.
  • Collaboration beats perfection. Good work gets done by talking things through, learning as you go, and staying flexible enough to improve over time.
