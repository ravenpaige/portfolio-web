Decode Intent with IANE

How to Uncover Hidden Meaning in Persuasive Messages Using AI

What Is IANE?

The Intent-Aware Narrative Engine (IANE) is a rhetorical analysis framework that helps you uncover the *why* behind the words. Unlike simple summarization tools, IANE focuses on motive, intent, audience posture, and narrative success conditions.

The MI-R-A-E Model

IANE is powered by five core questions:

  • Motive – What internal emotional or psychological drive is at play?
  • Intent – What outcome is the speaker trying to produce?
  • Relation – What posture does the speaker take toward the subject or audience?
  • Audience – Who is being addressed, and in what rhetorical frame?
  • Evaluation – What would success look like from the speaker’s perspective?

Try It Yourself

Paste any speech or article into your favorite language model and prompt it with:

Analyze the following using the MI-R-A-E model:
- Motive
- Intent
- Relation
- Audience
- Evaluation
[Insert text here]

Real Examples

  • Robert Reich – Used MI-R-A-E to reveal his call to unify progressives under a shared climate-democracy frame.
  • Donald Trump – Analysis showed dominance signaling, loyalty rituals, and self-branding embedded in his crypto speech.
  • Ronald Reagan – His iconic “Tear down this wall” speech layered strategic pressure over moral inspiration.

Why It Matters

IANE helps you move from content awareness to intent awareness. It reveals emotional leverage, power dynamics, and the symbolic structure of persuasive language—tools every communicator, analyst, or citizen should understand.
