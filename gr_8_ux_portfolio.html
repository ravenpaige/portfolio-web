About Me

I’m Terri Clark — a technical communicator, UX writer, and system integrator who specializes in clarifying complexity. I help teams understand what they’re building, why it matters, and how to communicate that clearly to the people who use it.

In a world where AI can generate content and code, my value lives upstream: I shape structure, uncover intent, and guide communication that aligns technical truth with human understanding. I bring system thinking to storytelling, and rhetorical awareness to documentation.

Whether I'm writing API documentation, building developer onboarding flows, mapping symbolic architectures, or collaborating with AI to co-design meaning — I focus on the one thing that doesn’t change: the need to understand.

I work remotely from Cassopolis, Michigan. I care about clarity, accessibility, and making technology serve people — not overwhelm them.

I'm open to full-time, part-time, or contract opportunities. I also consult on projects involving human-AI collaboration, doc system strategy, and narrative UX.
